Timely access to compatible blood donors is crucial during medical emergencies, yet existing systems often depend on informal channels such as social media, resulting in delays and unreliable information. BloodBridge is a smart, decentralized platform designed to overcome these challenges through automation, real-time communication, and integration with verified healthcare systems. It allows users to register as donors or recipients, enabling requestors to quickly locate and connect with nearby compatible donors. The platform also sends automated alerts via SMS or email, ensuring faster response times and better coordination. By streamlining the donor–recipient matching process, BloodBridge transforms the blood donation ecosystem into a more efficient, reliable, and life-saving network.
